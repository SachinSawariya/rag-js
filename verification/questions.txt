1. What company filed a lawsuit against YouTube in November 2006 because their website utube.com was being overloaded by people looking for YouTube?"
Answer: Universal Tube & Rollform Equipment (Universal Tube)

2. What was the exact square footage (in square metres) of the office park YouTube purchased in San Bruno in January 2016, and how much did it cost?"
Answer: 51,468 square metres (554,000 square feet) for $215 million

3. What was located directly below YouTube's early headquarters in San Mateo, California?
